Favorite Thing From This Summer

Attended a bachelorette party in Las Vegas

I have never been to Vegas before in my life and for some reason I thought instead of easing my way into it I should go all out and attend my cousins 4 day long bachelortte party with each day packed of activities.

What I learned from this trip

  1. Never plan a trip with 20 people in Vegas
  2. Apply sunscreen to the part in you hair
  3. Do not wear brand new shoes to Vegas beacuse you will have blisters for days and walk on the Vegas strip barefoot everywear becasue of the pain (not my brightest moment)
  4. Drinks are INSANELY expensive and I am not proud of my $75 Vodka Redbull (it did however come in a cool cup)...still not justified
